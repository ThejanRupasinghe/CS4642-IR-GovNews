11 Jan

President Mahinda Rajapakse paid an official visit to the Ministry of Power and Energy today.

President Mahinda Rajapakse paid an official visit to the Ministry of Power and Energy today. This inspection tour is the second such ministerial visit of the president.
The President was briefed on the present situation of the Ministry and its affiliated institutions, their victories and future plans.
Subsequently the President participated in the Ministries traditions and rituals. The President was joined on this occasion by Secretary to the Ministry of Power and Energy M.M.C Fernando, Chairman LECO Chandana Haputhantri, Chairman CEB Vidya Amarapala, and Chairman Sustainable Energy Authority D.M.D.O.C Disanayake.
