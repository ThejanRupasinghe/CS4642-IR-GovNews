15 Sep

CEB employees who have retired after 01.09.2014 may report to the work forthwith.

As an action to maintain a continuous supply of energy needed to electricity consumers, it has been decided to invite the employees of the following categories who have retired after 01.09.2014 by serving at Ceylon Electricity Board, to report to work.
Therefore, we kindly invite the employees of the above categories who worked in Ceylon Electricity Board and wish to render their services again to CEB, to report to work at the stations where they finally served.
