24 Hour service to solve Power consumers complaints instantly.

Measures have been taken by the Ministry of Power and Energy, Ceylon Electricity Board, and the Lanka Electric Co. To implement a 24 hour service to solve all consumer complaints with regard to electricity related issues under the directions of the Minister of Power and Energy Patali Champika Ranawaka.
Accordingly consumers can contact the Ministry of Power and Energy on the hotline 1901, The Ceylon Electricity Board on 1987, and Lanka Electric Co.
