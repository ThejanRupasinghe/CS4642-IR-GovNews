26 Jun

Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi sprinkled with water from hydropower reservoirs

Minister of Power and Renewable Energy Mr. Ranjith Siyambalapitiya entered into a vow at the Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi few months ago wishing for rainwater to fill the major reservoirs, for hydro power generation.
After receiving heavy rains as wished, the minister fulfilled the vow by sprinkling the Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi with water collected from seven hydro power reservoirs.
