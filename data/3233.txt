01 Oct

A group of visiting Japanese senate members met the minister of power and energy

A group of visiting Japanese senate members met the minister of power and energy Attorney Pavithra Wanniarachchi recently (28 Sept), and held extensive discussions on the developments in the power sector.
The Japanese delegation was headed by Eriko Yamatani while the other members of the delegation was Takashi Uto, Yoshitaka Saito, and Yuichi Mayama.
Along with the senate delegation Director General of the Senate Masayuki Kudo, the research officer of the Senate Masami Kawate, the secretary of the Japanese Senate Masayuki Yoshida and the Japanese Ambassador to Sri Lanka also participated in this discussion
